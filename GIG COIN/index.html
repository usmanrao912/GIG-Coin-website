Benefits using GigXCoin

GigX offers a range of benefits to users, toll operators, and other
stakeholders, including:

Instant Payments

GigX enables users to make toll payments and EV charging payments instantly.



Cost-Effective

GigX eliminates the need for expensive tolling infrastructure, such as toll booths, transponders, and back-end processing systems, which results in lower transaction fees for users and cost savings for toll operators.



Secure

GigX uses advanced blockchain technology to ensure that transactions are secure, transparent, and tamper-proof..



Accessible

GigX is accessible to everyone with a smartphone, making it easy for commuters and commercial transporters to make toll payments.

Eco-friendly

GigX eliminates the need for paper receipts and reduces carbon emissions associated with the production and transport of cash and other traditional toll payment methods.
